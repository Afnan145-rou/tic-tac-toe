How to play

  1. Tap a square to place your mark.
  2. Get three in a row to win. If all squares fill with no line, it’s a draw.
  3. Use Mode to switch between PvP and AI.
  4. Swap First Player toggles who starts the next round.

Tip: Navigate with Tab. Press Enter/Space to play a focused cell.
